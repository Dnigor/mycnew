Need Help?
  • To view a customer’s full profile click anywhere on the customer’s row.
  • Click the checkbox next to a customer to:
    • Print, create Labels or Export customer information.
    • File inactive customers by clicking Archive. To view archived customers, click “Archived” under the Filter option.
    • Delete customers by clicking Delete. To view deleted customers, click the trash can icon.
  • Identify a specific group of customers using Search, Filter, Sort options. For example quickly find all customers that live in your city by entering your city name in the search field.
  • To clear search or filter results, click the blue ‘X’.
  • Click the Notes tab to view and search customer notes. For example, if you entered “Teacher” in the notes of several customers, you can locate these customers by entering “Teacher” in the search box.
  • Click the Picture icon to view images of your customers.
