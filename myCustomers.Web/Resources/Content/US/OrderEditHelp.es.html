Need Help?
  • Quickly add products by entering the product name in the search field or by choosing products from the customer’s Previously Ordered, Recommended or Wish list> options.
  • Enter the delivery information. Recipient Name is the person who will be receiving the order if it will be someone other than the customer.
  • Track the Payment Status of the order.
  • Add a Note to the order to help you track and manage it.
  • Schedule an order Follow-up date with your customer. Order follow-ups will automatically appear on the Reminders page by due date.
  • Click Save and Review Order to proceed to the next page where you can create an Invoice and finalize the order.
  • If an invoice is not needed, click the Mark as Complete on this page to finalize the order.
