Need Help?
  • Reminders help you to stay organized and on track with personal reminders and automatically generated reminders such as Personal Website registrations or updates, business opportunity requests, customer order follow-ups, birthday and anniversary celebrations, host a party website requests, sample delivery and Personal Website Sample requests.
  • Click New Reminder to add a reminder.
  • Sort reminders using the Filter options.
  • Click the pencil icon to edit a Reminder.
  • Click the checkbox beside the reminder to close or complete it. Note: The next time you return to this page the reminder will no longer appear.
